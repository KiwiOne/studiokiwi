Microsites by Studio Kiwi

Whoever said that size doesn't matter was lying to you! Sometimes, the best way to communicate your message is with a trimmed back microsite that is 100% focused on your vision. What we are offering for £500 is a beautifully tailored one page website that is crafted to communicate the message you're looking to get heard. Once you provide us with the content and any guidelines you have regarding the message you are trying to communicate, we will pass it over to our dedicated design and development team to turn your idea into something tangible that could very well be the first step to making your idea a reality.
